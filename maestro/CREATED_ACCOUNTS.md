Accounts are created with the following pattern:

- Email: `njwandroid+MMDDYYYY_HHMMSS@gmail.com`
- Name: `Test User MMDDYYYY_HHMMSS`

3. Go to account settings
4. Click "Delete my account" at the bottom of the page
5. Confirm deletion
